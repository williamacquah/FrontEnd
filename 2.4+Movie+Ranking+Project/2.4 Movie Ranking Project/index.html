The best movies according to William

My top 3 movies of all-time

Exam

The film's appeal lies in its gripping storyline, suspenseful atmosphere, and thought-provoking themes. It keeps viewers on the edge of their seats as they try to unravel the mystery behind the exam and the true intentions of the characters. With its clever twists and turns, "Exam" challenges the audience to question their assumptions and examine the nature of ambition, morality, and human behavior.

Joker

The film's appeal lies in its compelling portrayal of Arthur's character, brilliantly brought to life by Joaquin Phoenix's haunting and award-winning performance. "Joker" offers a dark and gritty exploration of themes such as mental illness, social inequality, and the breakdown of empathy in society. Its atmospheric cinematography, haunting score, and immersive storytelling captivate audiences and draw them into Arthur's troubled world. The film challenges viewers to confront uncomfortable truths about the human condition while providing a gripping and emotionally resonant narrative. Additionally, its examination of the origins of one of pop culture's most iconic villains offers a fresh and compelling perspective that can leave a lasting impact on audiences.
